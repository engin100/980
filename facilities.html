Facilities

Below is a brief overview of the laboratory spaces used by ENGR100-980.

Soldering Lab

Workstations equipped with soldering irons and fume extractors for circuit board fabrication.

Computer Lab

CAEN computing stations for CAD design and PCB production, utilizing industry-standard software packages.

Research/Design Lab

Collaborative team workspaces for research and design.

Construction Lab

Tools and materials for building and assembling the final flight hardware.
